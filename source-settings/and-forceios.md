# \&forceios

Sender-Side Option! ([`&push`](push.md))

## Details

By default, iOS devices do not publish video to other guests in a group room.

{% hint style="danger" %}
iOS devices can publish **THREE (3)** video streams at any one time, before things explode.
{% endhint %}
